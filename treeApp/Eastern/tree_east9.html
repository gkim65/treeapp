Hop Tree

Ptelea trifoliata

Fun Facts!

Hop tree, ( Ptelea trifoliata ), also called wafer ash, tree or shrub of the rue family ( Rutaceae ), native to eastern North America. The hop tree is cultivated as an ornamental and is attractive to butterflies. The hop tree has a rounded crown and often features one or more crooked trunks with intertwining branches.
